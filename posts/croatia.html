Cycling the dragon.

Once again from 1st until 6th of October the Croatia's most beautiful sports postcard will be sent to the world, this year under the name CRO Race. As it did in the past four years company Top Sports Events will organize the race and once again prove excellence in organizing such an amazing sport event. As in previous years, we are expecting six stages, covering more than a thousand kilometers long route through Croatia. The race is listed at the UCI World Championship calendar, 2.1 category and will welcome the best cyclists and teams in the world. The greatest sports excitement and sights all around Croatia will be once again broadcasted on six continents.
